About Me

I'm software developer skilled in building various applications using front and back-end technologies. I love watching the project come to life as it's built piece by piece. I first got interested in coding from a couple of friends who had already taken a course at a local coding bootcamp. They told me that I seemed like someone who would enjoy and be good at coding because of my problem solving personality. I started by trying a pre-course work for said bootcamp and found that I really did have a knack for it. Shorty after, I started the course myself. Every project I worked on, I did not simply want it to be passing so I reached for stretch goals plus adding some of my own features into it. I enjoyed going over the top with them and learning how to give them that little bit of extra flair that I wanted. My abilities gradually improved and so did the depths of my projects. I'm currently continuing my learning by taking on projects for friends and family as well as building out ideas of my own while trying out new frameworks and languages such as React and C++. Prior to coding, I worked at Jimmy John's for 6+ years, 3 of which were in a management position. I loved being responsible and relied on to get the job done right. I made my way up to General Manager and realized 2 things: 1 is that I felt stuck in a job that didn't have much more room for growth past what I had already achieved and 2 is that I knew I could do more with my abilities than run a sandwich shop. This is what pushed me into looking for a new field, thus how my coding journey began.
