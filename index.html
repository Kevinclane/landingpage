About Me

I'm software developer skilled in building various applications using front and back-end technologies. I love watching the project come to life as it's built piece by piece. I first got interested in coding from a couple of friends who had already taken a course at a local coding bootcamp. They told me that I seemed like someone who would enjoy and be good at coding because of my problem solving personality. I started by trying a pre-course work for said bootcamp and found that I really did have a knack for it.
Shorty after, I started the course myself. During the course, we were often given a lecture in the morning about how to use new software and had to build projects with them in the afternoon. This led to us having to learn how to utilize new information very quickly in order to pass. For every project I worked on, I was not content just hitting the minimum requirements to pass, so I reached for stretch goals plus adding some of my own features into it. I enjoyed going over the top with them and learning how to give them that little bit of extra flair that I wanted. My abilities gradually improved and so did the depths of my projects.
I'm currently continuing my learning by taking on projects for friends and family as well as building out ideas of my own. My next big project is a Stock Market based app. Users will be able to spend fake money to invest in stocks in order to practice if their strategies will works or not before commiting real money into the market.
